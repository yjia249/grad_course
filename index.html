Graduate Selective Course

Introduction to Soft Matter and Biophysics

Research-paper-based teaching website

16 classes (90 minutes each): 1 opening class and 15 paper-centered topic lectures. Students can use this site during class and for post-class review.

Teaching Design

  • Start from one research paper per topic.
  • Explain what to calculate first and why.
  • Teach interpretation logic, not only final conclusions.
  • Build continuity from method to figure to physical message.

How to Use During Class

  1. Open the topic page from the lecture list below.
  2. Follow the 90-minute timeline section by section.
  3. Switch to the paper PDF for each figure discussion.
  4. Return to discussion prompts at the end.
